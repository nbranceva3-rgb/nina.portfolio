Gets distracted by the side panel. Can't figure out where to click to work with links. Eventually clicks "Select links", but this takes time (notes that the text button looks unclickable). When moving links, she  appreciates the confirmation popup.

Due to the filter chips, she doesn't understand the functionality at first (they're bright and immediately catch the eye — as if they activate links). She ignores the main action button for a long time because it looks like text. Doesn't read the explanatory tooltip on hover. Notes that "Open all tabs" would be clearer.

To proceed with purchase, she expected to click the inactive Focus mode button. When that didn't work, she went to Settings: "I'm used to being able to do and find everything in settings." Only then clicked the Focus mode icon in the top right.

During purchase she didn't understand the difference between monthly and yearly. Expected to switch between plans within a single card. Also expected the same features listed across all cards, just more in premium.

Doesn't understand what Focus mode actually does. Expected that upon purchase there would be a more detailed explanation, or a window showing how the feature works graphically. "If it were like at the beginning (onboarding), it would be clearer. I don't want to read tooltips — it's tiring."

Mechanically turning the feature on/off caused no issues.
