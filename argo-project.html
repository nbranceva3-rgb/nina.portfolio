Overview

Greek coffee, groceries & more

Argo is a Greek coffee shop and grocery store that needed a full digital presence — from an online shop to in-store ordering experiences.

The project involved designing desktop and mobile versions of the e-shop, a Grab & Go ordering flow, a recipes page, gift cards page, and a loyalty rewards programme.

E-shop Grab & Go Gift Cards Recipes Rewards Mobile & Desktop

The Challenge

One brand, many touchpoints

Argo needed a cohesive digital ecosystem that felt warm and on-brand across very different use cases — from a quick in-store pickup to a full online grocery shop.

The challenge was to design flows that feel intuitive for both regulars and first-time visitors, while keeping the visual identity consistent throughout.

Design Direction

Warm tones, playful illustrations, and a clear hierarchy — making every interaction feel like a visit to the store itself.

Scope of Work

What was designed

A full suite of screens across desktop and mobile, covering the entire customer journey from discovery to checkout.
