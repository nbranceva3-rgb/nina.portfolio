The Problem

Control over the grind

People who take fitness seriously don't want to be handed a rigid plan — they want ownership. They want to decide when to train, what to eat, and how to track their progress on their own terms.

Most fitness platforms dictate the journey. Pleasant Pain was built around the opposite idea: give users the tools, the products, and the freedom to make it their own.

Core Insight

The pain is the point — but only when it's chosen. Users want to feel in control of their discomfort, not subjected to it by someone else's schedule.

The Solution

A brand that earns its sweat

Pleasant Pain is a subscription platform for workout programmes and nutrition plans, with a merch shop at its core. I designed the full brand identity — logo, graphic elements, and visual system — alongside key product screens and flows.

The scope ranged from the first impression on the homepage to the last step of checkout, and even a browser extension that lets users shop without leaving their current tab.

01 Brand identity — logo, graphic elements, and visual system built from scratch
02 Homepage and static pages — communicating the product offering and brand tone
03 Merch shop, cart, and full checkout flow — designed for speed and clarity
04 Registration and login screens — seamless onboarding into the ecosystem
05 Browser extension — lets users browse and purchase merch without leaving their current page
